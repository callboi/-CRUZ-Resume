Tribute to Bruce Wayne

Bruce Wayne

In Frank Miller's comic book "The Dark Knight Returns," Batman/Bruce Wayne delivers a eulogy for a fallen friend. The words he shares are not only a touching tribute but also a profound reflection on how to express love, support, and gratitude for those who have touched our lives.

Bruce was a beacon of light who believed in the power of love and kindness. They taught us that it isn't the length of our days that truly matter but the impact we leave on those we encounter. As we stand here together, let us remember Bruce's unwavering spirit, their infectious laughter, and their undeniable love that transcends time.
